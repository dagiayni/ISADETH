About ISAD Ethiopia

At ISAD, we believe that transforming agriculture begins with informed farmers. Our mission is to close the knowledge gap by partnering with agricultural researchers and expert farmers to offer high-quality, affordable agricultural education and support services tailored to the Ethiopian context.

  • Accessible Agricultural Education
  • Locally Relevant & Expert-Led Content
  • Empowering Farmers & Agripreneurs

Our Story

ISAD was founded in 2023 by Eyuel Tegenu and Abrham Mulatu, two passionate entrepreneurs who shared a deep love for agriculture but struggled to find the right information to start their journey in Ethiopia. This challenge sparked the idea for ISAD – a platform dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality agricultural education to new and existing farmers, agripreneurs, and researchers. Since then, we have been working tirelessly to build a hub of knowledge that empowers individuals to transform agriculture through innovation and expertise

Our mission

Vision 1

To be Africa's leading platform for agricultural transformation by equipping individuals and communities with the knowledge and tools needed to build sustainable, productive, and profitable agricultural systems.

Vision 2

We envision a future where farmers and agripreneurs, regardless of their background or location, can access high-quality agricultural training, apply it practically, and succeed in farming and agribusiness.

Our mission

  • To make agricultural education accessible and affordable to everyone
  • To integrate traditional practices with modern technology and research
  • To promote sustainability and productivity in farming systems
  • To support farmers and agribusinesses from seed to scale
  • To empower youth and women to take leadership roles in agriculture
