About ISAD Ethiopia

At ISAD Ethiopia, we are committed to transforming agriculture through innovation, sustainability, and expertise. Our mission is to empower farmers, agribusinesses, and stakeholders with the tools and knowledge they need to thrive in an evolving agricultural landscape.

  • Driving Agricultural Innovation
  • Supporting Sustainable Farming Practices
  • Empowering Local Farmers and Agribusinesses
  • Providing Expert Training and Consultation
  • Leveraging Technology for Smarter Farming
  • Enhancing Market Linkages

Our Story

ISAD was founded in 2023 by Eyuel Tegenu and Abrham Mulatu, two passionate entrepreneurs who shared a deep love for agriculture but struggled to find the right information to start their journey in Ethiopia. This challenge sparked the idea for ISAD – a platform dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality agricultural education to new and existing farmers, agripreneurs, and researchers. Since then, we have been working tirelessly to build a hub of knowledge that empowers individuals to transform agriculture through innovation and expertise

Our mission

At ISAD, we believe that knowledge is the key to agricultural transformation. Our mission is to spread efficient, practical, and research-backed agricultural knowledge to boost productivity, improve food security, and create sustainable farming practices across Ethiopia. We aim to make high-quality agricultural education accessible to everyone, from beginners looking to start their journey to experienced farmers wanting to scale their agribusinesses.

Our mission

Over the next 5 to 10 years, ISAD envisions a future where agricultural education is seamlessly integrated with technology. Our goal is to:

  • Develop technologically advanced learning systems that make agricultural education more interactive and accessible.
  • Educate and empower our community with cutting-edge farming techniques
  • Become a key partner for farmers, agripreneurs, and agricultural enthusiasts looking to start or scale their ventures.
